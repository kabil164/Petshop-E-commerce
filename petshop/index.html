Our Story

Dedicated to pet excellence since 2010

JoyNPets was founded by a team of veterinarians and pet enthusiasts who believed pets deserve the very best. What began as a small boutique has grown into a premier destination for discerning pet owners.

We carefully curate our collection, working directly with manufacturers who share our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical production. Every product in our store meets our rigorous standards for safety, durability, and design.

Our knowledgeable staff are always available to help you find the perfect products for your pet's unique needs.

Our Philosophy

Quality, care, and compassion

At JoyNPets, we believe pets are family. That's why we're committed to providing products that enhance their wellbeing while making pet ownership easier and more enjoyable for you.

We carefully vet every manufacturer and supplier to ensure they meet our high standards for quality, safety, and ethical production. Our team includes certified pet nutritionists and trainers who test and evaluate every product we carry.

Beyond products, we're dedicated to pet education. Our in-store workshops and online resources help pet owners make informed decisions about their companions' care.

Meet Our Team

Passionate pet professionals

Our staff includes certified veterinary technicians, professional groomers, and trained animal behaviorists who bring their expertise to every customer interaction.

Founder and CEO Dr. Sarah Johnson, DVM, leads our product selection team, ensuring every item meets veterinary standards for safety and nutrition.

Whether you're visiting our flagship store or shopping online, you'll receive personalized recommendations from our knowledgeable team members.
